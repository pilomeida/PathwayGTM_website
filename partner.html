The Partners Circle

Expanding What's Possible

Pathway's core expertise covers the full GTM lifecycle — but every client engagement has its own unique requirements. The Partners Circle lets us bring in the right specialist at the right time, without compromising the cohesion of the engagement.

Partners are selected for depth of expertise, alignment with Pathway's execution-first philosophy, and ability to integrate into client engagements without disruption.

What We Look For

Partner Specializations

We're building the Partners Circle across several specialist domains that complement Pathway's GTM core.

Specialists in GTM analytics, revenue intelligence, data infrastructure, and business intelligence for B2B revenue organizations.

GTM Technology

CRM, MAP, sales engagement, CS platforms, and integration specialists who can implement and optimize the technology layer of GTM.

How It Works

Partnership Model

01

Expression of Interest

Share your background, specialization, and the types of engagements you'd be interested in supporting. We review all applications and respond to relevant profiles.

02

Alignment Conversation

A short conversation to understand your depth of expertise, working style, and how your capabilities would complement Pathway's engagement model.

03

Partners Circle Onboarding

Accepted partners are onboarded to the Partners Circle — briefed on Pathway's methodology, client engagement norms, and how collaboration typically works in practice.

04

Engagement Activation

When a client engagement calls for your specialization, Pathway reaches out to activate you — with clear scope, timeline, and integration into the overall engagement structure.
